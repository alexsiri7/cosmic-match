Swap gems. Match three. Create cosmic cascades.

Cosmic Match is a space-themed match-3 puzzle game where you swap adjacent tiles to create matches of three or more. Chain your matches for explosive cascade combos that send your score into the stratosphere.

GAMEPLAY
• Tap two adjacent gems to swap their positions
• Match 3 or more gems of the same color to clear them
• Gems fall under gravity after each clear, setting up new matches
• Chain multiple matches in a row for cascade bonuses
• Beat your personal best — score is saved automatically

FEATURES
🌌 Space-themed board with colorful gem tiles
⭐ Smooth animations and physics-based cascades
💾 Auto-save — pick up exactly where you left off
🔒 No account required — just download and play
📵 Full offline support — no internet needed
🎮 Simple controls, satisfying depth

BUILT WITH LOVE
Cosmic Match is built using Flutter and the Flame game engine. Simple to pick up, with the depth to keep you coming back — a game you can play for 2 minutes or 2 hours.
